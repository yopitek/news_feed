The announcement has sparked discussions within the tech community about the implications for legacy systems still relying on Telnet. While some users have expressed concern over the potential disruptions, others view the move as a necessary step toward enhancing cybersecurity. The IETF's decision is expected to accelerate the phase-out of Telnet in favor of more secure protocols, reinforcing the importance of data protection in an increasingly digital world.

The lift can carry up to four boats at a time, with each gondola holding a maximum of 20 tons. The project was part of a larger initiative to revitalize the canal system in central Scotland and promote tourism in the region.
